# Vote(Magnifier)

### What is magnifier NFT?
The magnifier is an NFT that represent voting rights of player on the BurnYou battles and record the result of voting.

### Why is a magnifier NFT needed for voting?
- Because the cost of creating numerous cryptocurrency wallets is none, a certificate would be needed to ensure the fairness of each vote and prevent unfaithful voting.
- The magnifier NFT represents the voter's level of appreciation.
- The magnifier NFT serves as the proof of the voter's mining.（Vote2Earn）

### How do you use the magnifier NFT to vote?
- Each wallet could only carry one magnifier NFT.
- The magnifier NFT could only vote once in one battle.


### How can I claim a magnifier NFT?
- Minting by purchase
- Minting by whitelist(free)

### Can the magnifier NFT be transferred?
No.

### Are all magnifiers NFT the same?
When magnifier NFT initial minted were all the same, magnifier may change by participating in vote event. The changes in magnifier could be categorized as upgrades and downgrades, which impact the voting weight and mining rewards.

### What actions could determine the change of Magnifiers?
- The number of votes
- The number of correct votes
- The number of incorrect votes
