Creating battle to help winner NFT(s) attract attention in global market. Survival of the fittest, BurnYou reduces market supply and concentrate on  high-quality NFT(s).

### What does mean by “be burned”?
Burning an NFT is not like physical destruction in real world but simply is the smart contract where the NFT would be sent to a '0 address' without a private key, so that irretrievable.

Is Ethereum the only blockchain accepted by BurnYou for battles?
BurnYou is a decentralized application built in Ethereum ecosystem.
Authorizing and staking your NFT would occur on Ethereum Layer1, all  battles and transaction would take place on Ethereum L2 ZK-Sync.

If your NFT is on ZK-Sync, you could directly start battling and trading, enjoying the cost-effectiveness of Ethereum Layer 2 network.
