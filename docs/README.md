# Overview

### What is BurnYou?
BurnYou is a block-chain platform that operates NFT battle events, facilitates NFT trading and intellectual property (IP) incubation.
<br>Our events include NFT one-on-one and group battles,with the results being determined by a public voting system.The losing NFTs will be BURNED whilst the winning NFTs receive promotional funding and community attention. $Burn tokens would be distributed to both side accordingly.

### What is the motivation behind BurnYou?
BurnYou promotes premium NFTs by hosting battle events and incubates new IP that is owned by a new generation of young people with in the block-chain world.

### What are the key benefits of BurnYou?

- As a player:
  <br>You will increase the value of your winning NFTs or even the entire NFT collection by receiving promotion fees from losing opponent. The NFTs sfrom your opponents would be "burned" and its price would reduced to zero,and would be not longer be transferable.
  <br>You do not need to pay any registration fees nor any other costs, (gas fee is given by the way). Further you would get some AIRDROP as a sign of our appreciation for your early participation.
- As a NFT voters, you would vote for your favourite NFTs in the battle field. Please join our free Mint Event of our magnifier NFT to enjoy 'Vote to Earn'.
- As a NFT collectors,you can select and buy the NFTs that you think has great potential, at a current low price in BurnYou.

### Can BurnYou be upgraded or changed?
BurnYou has no admin key, but governance votes would take place if a undergo rule needs to be changed or upgraded. Otherwise, the smart contract code remains unchanged.

### How to use BurnYou?
First, you would visit BurnYou.io and connect your wallet to authorise your NFTs in order to wait for and accept challenges. At the same time, you can participate in voting events of the battles to look for dark horse NFTs.

How many battle modes are there in BurnYou？
- NFT 1v1 Battle
  <br>Battle player could host arena wait for opponents or make a challenge directly to the exist arena. Battle result is determined by public voting.

- Group battle between NFT collections
  <br>On the basis of 1v1 Battle, any NFTs from same collection could engage battle to rescue the losing teammate NFT within 24 hours. If no teammate NFT rescues, losing NFT(s) would be executed as defeat and all losing NFTs would be burned.

- Bid & Collect player
  <br>Bid&Collect player could make a fixed 4% markup each raise-hand to bid in-battle NFT(s). Just like making a bet, the amount of markup raise-hand(N*4%) would be ALL contributed to promotion pool of winner NFT if your bidding NFT lost, however the base amount 100% would return to you. In the other words, only 4%(s) would be lost or be used to promote. As BC player you could think further, you make 4% profit each correct raise-hand if your bidding NFT won the battle.


- Promotion pool for winner NFT(s)
  <br>Any X(Twitter) users could change their X profile picture to the winner NFT image for more than 24hours in order to earn the promotion fee that according to the percentage of your followers in the total followers pool.

### Will I incur losses?
- As an non-custodial system, all tokens sent to the protocol will be stored and managed through algorithms, without interference from any individual or institution. This means your funds will be solely governed by the rules specified in the smart contract code.
- Participating in NFT battles, loser NFT(s) would lost entire value of their NFT(s) however certain amount of token would be ready to claim as battle compensation, while winner NFT(s) have potential to appreciate in value according to the number of votes and the amount of promotion fund pool.

Please note that, while we have conducted a very thorough review of our system, we cannot entirely eliminate the possibility of player losses due to hackers or system vulnerabilities (please refer to the disclaimer).
