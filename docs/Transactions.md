# Transactions (appreciation)

### Why the value of my NFT Battle could increase after winning?
Because there is a unique bidding auction mode taking place on BurnYou. Similar to a real-world auction, Bid&Collect player could make a fixed 4% markup each raise-hand to bid in-battle NFT(s). Just like making a bet, the amount of markup raise-hand(N*4%) would be ALL contributed to promotion pool of winner NFT if your bidding NFT lost, however the base amount 100% would return to you. In the other words, only 4%(s) would be lost or be used to promote. As BC player you could think further, you make 4% profit each correct raise-hand if your bidding NFT won the battle.

### What should I do if the NFT I bid on loses the Battle?
Unfortunately the additional amount you bid would be entirely placed into the promotion pool for promoting NFT winner on Twitter(X).
<br>On the other hand ,you would make some profits if you made a correct bid and winner NFT would receive promotion on Twitter(X).



### What is promotion pool?
Promotion pool is BurnYou's unique product to increase NFT liquidity. Battle players could either benefit from the appreciation of winner NFT or contribute their funds to winner NFT.

### Do bid&collect players receive mining rewards?
Yes,bid&collect players would receive mining rewards.You are welcome to be a bid&collect player and a battle player in the Battle at the same time, and even a voter player. Please remember that all player roles on BurnYou come with mining rewards, so please have a try ALL roles on BurnYou to maximize your earning rewards.
